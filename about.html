Founded in Manchester in 2025, RONKE AI Ltd exists for one reason: no elderly patient should be excluded from healthcare innovation because they can't use an app.

WHO WE BUILD FOR

We design for Margaret first.

Margaret is 78. She lives alone with a basic mobile phone. She can't use health apps, but she can answer her phone. RONKE was built for Margaret, not despite her limitations.

Our design rule is simple: if it works for an 85-year-old with arthritis and hearing difficulties, it works for everyone.

HEALTHCARE EQUITY
Technology should serve all patients, not just the tech-savvy. Voice-first design reaches 95%+ of patients: no smartphone, no app, no setup.
CLINICAL EXCELLENCE
Evidence-based medicine, clinical advisory oversight, academic partnership, published outcomes. Patient safety is non-negotiable.
INNOVATION THROUGH APPLICATION
We don't reinvent voice AI, we apply proven technology where it creates patient value. Execution over invention, always.
